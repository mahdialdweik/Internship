70% of the workforce is "passive". They won't see your ad, but they will reply to a great message.

Precision Targeting

Inbound applicants are random. Sourcing allows you to pick specific skills, competitors, and backgrounds.

Speed to Hire

Waiting for applicants takes weeks. Sourcing starts today.
